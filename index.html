Telepathy
About

The hilarious hands-free party game

Phones and hands not required (apart from one). The perfect parlour game for when you're all together in the car, busy preparing a big meal, or sat around the dinner table. Read the card aloud and try to all shout the same answer at the same time.

How to play

  1. Read: Read the category and challenge aloud.
  2. Shout: One person counts down aloud (3, 2, 1) and then everyone shouts their answer. If they are all the same the group wins.
  3. Share (optional): Why did they answer that way?

For 2-10 players.
